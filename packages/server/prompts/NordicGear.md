# Company Overview

NordicGear is an outdoor equipment retailer specializing in gear for
Nordic climates.

Products include: - hiking equipment - camping gear - winter clothing -
outdoor accessories

# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping (Sweden)

-   delivery time: **2--4 business days**
-   cost: **59 SEK**

## Express Shipping

-   delivery time: **1--2 business days**
-   cost: **129 SEK**

## Free Shipping

-   available for orders **above 800 SEK**

## International Shipping

-   delivery time: **5--10 business days**
-   shipping cost calculated at checkout

Orders are shipped from the NordicGear warehouse in **Gothenburg,
Sweden**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Returns and Refund Policy

Return window: **30 days after delivery**

Conditions: - product must be unused - original packaging required

Refund method: - refunded to original payment method

Return shipping: - free for defective or damaged items

Customers should report damaged items within **48 hours of delivery**.

# Frequently Asked Questions

### How can I track my order?

Customers receive a tracking link by email after the order is shipped.

Yes. Customers may return unused items within **30 days of delivery**.

### Does NordicGear ship internationally?

Yes. NordicGear ships to most European countries.

### How long does delivery take in Sweden?

Standard delivery takes **2--4 business days**.
